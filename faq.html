Drive Thrive provides full wrap unlimited third party liability insurance without a maximum cap. However damage fee applies when the member is involved in an incident (including but not limited to a collision or misuse of the vehicle) Member will be responsible for a standard insurance deductible payment of up to Rs. 10,000. The Member will be obligated to pay the repair cost (up to Rs. 10,000). The payment owed will be deducted from the security deposit. Should the security deposit not cover the payment (in the case the Member has accrued late return, excess km, etc charges) the balance of the payment owed will be made electronically by the Member.

We take a small, fully refundable security deposit of Rs. 5,000 for every booking. In the rare case of damage to the vehicle, we deduct the cost of it from the above mentioned deposit. In case of higher damage, your maximum liability is limited to Rs 10,000 and we request you to pay the difference above the Rs 5,000 deposit. Under normal situations (no damage to vehicle) you will get the security deposit amount (after adjusting for extra KM charges if applicable) back in your bank account (It can take 3-5 days to reflect in the back account).
Of course, drinking under influence of alcohol or other substance makes the insurance void, and in such cases user is liable to pay up to full extent of the damage.
